Design Club was started by my buddy Oskar Radon and I to bring the design community closer together at the Pacific Northwest College of Art. We left it open to everyone, regardless of major and anyone outside of PNCA was welcome. We have ran nine meetings so far; eight were collaborative poster making sessions and one Blender workshop led by Oskar. Our core ideas were fast and experimental, focusing on discovery and sharing of new techniques rather than finished products.

We are currently in the process of creating a brand identity. We will package the identity and format to send to anyone wanting to start their own Design Club in pursuit of community driven global domination.
